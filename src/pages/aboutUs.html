About Us

Geeks 4 Gadgets is an online based store that distributes gadgets countrywide at little to no cost.
This company was established in 2007 by the late David Paulse with the aim of bridging the gap in the electronics industry,
since it was not easy back then to buy electronics online and having them delivered on time.
